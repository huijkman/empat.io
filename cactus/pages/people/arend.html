How can you help {{ name }}?

For designers

Design for easy fullfilling tasks People who depend on spoken texts only don't have a visual overview of a webpage or an app. They start at the top of the page and from there on they continue reading the page. By removing unnecessary information, create a clear website/app hierachy and focus on easy navigation to tasks you can help that person accomplish their task.

  • Design for the (top)tasks of the user
  • How to create a intuitive website information architecture

For developers

Develop screenreader friendly. Make users with a screenreader happy by building extra assistence and shortcuts for them. Also experience the use of your website/app with a screenreader.

  • How to add extra assistence for a screenreader in your website/app (Android | iOS | Web | Windows Phone)
  • Test with the build in screenreader of your operating system (Android | OS X/iOS | Windows | Windows Phone)
